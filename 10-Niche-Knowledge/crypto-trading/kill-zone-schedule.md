# Kill Zone Schedule

> ICT session timing for trade entries. Used by /technical-analyst to score kill zone confluence.

---

## Kill Zone Times (All Times EST)

| Session | Kill Zone Window | Peak Activity | Character |
|---------|-----------------|---------------|-----------|
| **Asia** | 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM | 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM | Consolidation, range-building. Often sets the liquidity pool that London sweeps. |
| **London** | 2:00 AM - 5:00 AM | 3:00 AM - 5:00 AM | Highest probability session. Often sets the daily high or low. Strongest moves. |
| **New York** | 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM | 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM | Reverses or extends London. Major news releases during this window. |

## Kill Zone Trading Rules

1. **Prioritize London and NY kill zones.** These have the highest probability setups.
2. **Asia kill zone setups** require 1 additional confluence beyond the normal minimum (4 instead of 3 for MEDIUM).
3. **Outside kill zones:** Setups are valid only if they score HIGH confidence (5+ confluences).
4. **News events:** Check economic calendar before entering during NY session. Major releases (FOMC, NFP, CPI) invalidate technical setups — do not signal within 30 minutes of a major release.
5. **Crypto trades:** Kill zones apply primarily to Forex. Crypto trades can be taken outside kill zones but London and NY sessions still have higher probability due to institutional activity.

## Weekly Schedule Notes

| Day | Notes |
|-----|-------|
| Monday | Often rangebound. Market digesting weekend crypto moves. Forex often sets the weekly range. |
| Tuesday-Thursday | Highest probability days. Most institutional flow. Best days for signals. |
| Friday | Reduced activity after NY close. Forex positions often squared before weekend. Crypto continues. |
| Saturday-Sunday | Forex closed. Crypto only. Lower liquidity = wider spreads = higher risk. Reduce confidence by 1 level. |
