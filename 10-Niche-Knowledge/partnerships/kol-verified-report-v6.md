## VERDICT KEY
- **VERIFIED PAYS** = Multiple independent confirmations of real payouts
- **PAYMENT ISSUES** = Real platform but reports of delays, locks, or selective denial
- **SCAM WARNING** = Multiple reports of non-payment, fund locking, or fraud
- **UNVERIFIED** = No independent payment proof found (marketing claims only)
- **LEGIT BUT LOW** = Platform is real but commission/salary claims are inflated
