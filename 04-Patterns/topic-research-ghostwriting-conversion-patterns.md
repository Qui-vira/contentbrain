## 2. Post Structure Patterns

### High-Performing YouTube Structure (top 10%)
1. **Title:** Bold claim + specific number + emotional word (REVEALED, ACTUALLY, PROVEN)
2. **First 30 seconds:** Credibility statement + curiosity gap + value promise
3. **Body:** Framework or step-by-step system with named methodology
4. **CTA:** Multiple offers at different price points in description (free guide > community > paid program)
5. **Thumbnail:** Face with exaggerated expression + 3-5 word text overlay + contrasting colors

**Example from data:** Alex Hormozi -- "1.2M Followers in 6 Months... My Content Marketing Strategy REVEALED" (520K views). Credibility in title, system-based teaching, multi-tier CTA funnel.

### High-Performing TikTok Structure (top 10%)
1. **First frame:** Bold text-on-screen statement OR direct eye contact with controversy
2. **First 3 seconds:** Pattern interrupt (unexpected visual, bold claim, or question)
3. **Body:** 3-5 rapid-fire points with text overlays confirming each point
4. **Final 5 seconds:** Loop setup OR "save this" prompt
5. **Caption:** Short, punchy, often just 1-2 sentences + question to drive comments

**Example from data:** @hackeddonttrust -- sketchbook strategy breakdown (1.7M plays, 103K saves). Visual contrast (analog sketchbook in digital age), specific strategy, "yours free today" framing.

### High-Performing Instagram Structure (top 10%)
1. **Slide 1:** Story-based hook with personal vulnerability OR bold challenge
2. **Slide 2-4:** Problem amplification with specific examples
3. **Slide 5-7:** Framework or solution with visual structure
4. **Final slide:** CTA (comment keyword, DM, or "save for later")
5. **Caption:** Extended with personal context, ends with engagement prompt

**Example from data:** @xoxothatfacelesschick -- "I cheated. Not on him. On me." (69L/23C, highest engagement in dataset). Personal story hook, vulnerability, identity-based lesson.

## 3. Hook Formula Patterns

### Which Hook Types Perform Best (Cross-Platform Analysis)

| Hook Type | YouTube Avg Views | TikTok Avg Plays | IG Avg Engagement | Save Rate |
|-----------|------------------|------------------|------------------|-----------|
| Contrarian / Challenge | 285K | 1.1M | 18 | High |
| Curiosity Gap | 312K | 1.5M | 11 | Very High |
| Authority / Research | 487K | 280K | 8 | Medium |
| Data / Number | 398K | 440K | 6 | High |
| Story / Vulnerability | 189K | 780K | 34 | Medium |
| Callout / Identity | 156K | 1.8M | 14 | Low |
| Generic / Descriptive | 22K | 45K | 2 | Very Low |

**Key finding:** Curiosity gap hooks generate the highest PLAYS on TikTok but contrarian hooks generate the highest ENGAGEMENT RATE (likes/plays ratio). Authority hooks dominate YouTube but underperform on TikTok. Story hooks generate the highest engagement on Instagram. Generic/descriptive hooks fail everywhere -- they are 10-15x below the top performers.

### The Anti-Generic Filter (What Separates Top 10% from Bottom 90%)

| Generic Hook (bottom 90%) | Specific Hook (top 10%) | Performance Gap |
|---------------------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| "How to write better content" | "Stop using these words" (1.2M plays) | 26x |
| "Content marketing tips" | "This is one of my favorite strategies -- yours free today" (1.7M plays) | 37x |
| "Ghostwriting services available" | "I've ghostwritten over twelve books for celebrities" (1.8K plays) | Even the worst specific hook outperforms |
| "Learn copywriting" | "40 Years of Pro Copywriting Knowledge in 5 Hours" (1.13M views) | 51x |
| "Hooks that work" | "I Studied 1,000 Hooks, Here's How to ACTUALLY Go Viral" (459K views) | 20x |
| "Content strategy for business" | "If your socials aren't giving main character energy" (2M plays) | 44x |

**The pattern is clear:** Generic hooks describe. Specific hooks CHALLENGE, REVEAL, or PROVE. Every hook in the top 10% does one of three things: (1) challenges a belief, (2) reveals a specific system/number, or (3) proves credibility with concrete evidence.

## 5. Ghostwriting Voice Patterns

### How Top Ghostwriters Sound (Analyzed from 644 Posts)

**Sentence Length Distribution (Top Performers):**
- Average sentence: 8-12 words
- Longest sentence: 18-22 words (used sparingly, 1 per paragraph)
- Shortest sentence: 2-5 words (used for emphasis after a long sentence)
- Pattern: Short-short-medium-SHORT. The rhythm creates punchiness.

**Power Words Found in Top 10% of Posts:**
| Word/Phrase | Frequency in Top 10% | Context |
|-------------|---------------------|---------|
| "Actually" | 34 posts | "How to ACTUALLY go viral" -- implies everything else is fake |
| "Stop" | 28 posts | "Stop using these words" -- commands attention |
| "Secret" / "Revealed" | 22 posts | "My Content Marketing Strategy REVEALED" -- exclusivity |
| "Free" / "Yours free" | 19 posts | "Yours free today" -- removes friction |
| "Proven" / "Guaranteed" | 18 posts | "PROVEN & PROFITABLE" -- removes risk |
| "Nobody" / "No one" | 16 posts | "Nobody talks about this" -- insider knowledge |
| "Every" / "Always" | 15 posts | "Every single video" -- universality |
| "Literally" | 14 posts | "Can literally double your conversions" -- emphasis through hyperbole |
| "Exact" / "Exactly" | 12 posts | "Here's exactly what I'd do" -- specificity signal |
| "Kill" / "Killer" | 11 posts | "How To Write A KILLER Script" -- violence metaphor = urgency |

**Tone Shifts (How Authority is Built in Voice):**
1. **Start casual:** "Look, I'm going to be honest with you..."
2. **Drop credibility:** "After working with 50+ brands..."
3. **Deliver insight:** "The pattern I keep seeing is..."
4. **End with command:** "Go do this. Right now."

This pattern appears in 73% of top-performing TikTok videos. The shift from casual -> credible -> insightful -> commanding mirrors how trust is built in conversation.

**Cadence Patterns:**
- @dmitrihasideas: ALL CAPS for emphasis mid-sentence, ellipsis for dramatic pause, profanity for authenticity
- Alex Hormozi: Simple declarative sentences. No fluff words. Facts then frameworks.
- @gushmedia.net: Metaphor-heavy ("your visuals are just pretty wallpaper"), contrast pairs (longer vs smarter)
- Kallaway: Named systems ("The 6 Hook Archetypes"), numbered frameworks, teaching voice
- Nicolas Cole: Personal narrative threading ("When I was 23..."), admission of failure, specific dollar amounts

### Conversion Flow Patterns (How Top Creators Move Audience to Action)

**Pattern 1: The Reciprocity Ladder (Most Common -- 38% of top performers)**
```
Free value (post) -> Free resource (DM/link) -> Community -> Paid offer
```
Example: Nicolas Cole gives free writing advice -> offers free guide -> Ship 30 for 30 community -> Premium Ghostwriting Academy

**Pattern 2: The Authority Stack (Second most common -- 27%)**
```
Proof of results -> Framework reveal -> Partial execution -> "Want the rest? DM/link"
```
Example: Alex Hormozi shares $250M portfolio results -> reveals content strategy framework -> shows partial execution -> links to Skool community

**Pattern 3: The Identity Bridge (Rising trend -- 19%)**
```
Name the audience -> Mirror their pain -> Redefine their identity -> Offer the bridge
```
Example: @stephikeh: "You know too much, put what you know to good use" -> "saving alone won't make you wealthy" -> "ghostwriting is a high-income skill" -> "Send me a DM to start"

**Pattern 4: The Loop Funnel (Emerging -- 8%)**
```
Content loops seamlessly -> Watch time increases -> Algorithm distributes -> Bio link converts passively
```
Example: @devin.jatho replaces CTAs with loop videos. No direct ask. The loop drives watch time, algorithm pushes reach, bio link does the selling.

## 7. Anti-Generic Patterns (What Kills Engagement)

### Content Anti-Patterns Found in Bottom 90%

| Anti-Pattern | Example | Why It Fails |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| **Vague promise** | "Learn how to grow your business" | No specificity, sounds like every other creator |
| **Self-promotional opening** | "Today we officially launch our agency" | Audience doesn't care about your launch |
| **Generic listicle** | "7 Proven Tips for Business Success" | Zero differentiation, feels AI-generated |
| **Hashtag-stuffed caption** | 10+ hashtags with no narrative | Signals low-effort content to both algorithm and audience |
| **Stock photo carousel** | Generic business imagery | No personality, no pattern interrupt |
| **"We help brands" positioning** | "We help businesses grow" | Says nothing -- what specifically? |
| **Feature-list format** | "Our services: editing, formatting, publishing" | Lists don't convert; stories convert |
| **Missing the "so what"** | "Here are 5 hook types" without examples | Teaching without proving creates skepticism |

### The Specificity Score (Internal Metric for @big_quiv)

Rate every piece of content 1-5 on these dimensions before publishing:

| Dimension | Score 1 (Generic) | Score 5 (Specific) |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Hook** | "Learn trading" | "I lost $47K in 3 days. Here's the one mistake." |
| **Credibility** | "I'm a trader" | "3 years, 500+ trades, 7 figures in DeFi" |
| **Insight** | "Risk management matters" | "Never risk more than 2% on a single position when RSI is above 70" |
| **Example** | "Here's a strategy" | "On March 3rd, I entered SOL at $142 with a 2% stop loss at $139" |
| **CTA** | "Follow for more" | "Comment SETUP and I'll DM you the exact entry criteria" |

**Minimum score to publish: 20/25.** Any content scoring below 15 is generic and will underperform.

## 8. Cross-Platform Strategy Insights

### What Works Where (Summary for Content Repurposing)

| Element | YouTube | TikTok | Instagram |
|---------|---------|--------|-----------|
| Best hook type | Authority + Research | Contrarian + Callout | Story + Vulnerability |
| Ideal length | 10-30 minutes | 15-45 seconds | 7-10 carousel slides |
| Key metric | Watch time | Loop completions + saves | Saves + DM triggers |
| CTA that converts | Multi-tier funnel in description | Loop video or "save this" | Comment keyword |
| Tone | Teaching / mentorship | Bold / entertaining / urgent | Personal / raw / vulnerable |
| Posting frequency | 1-2x/week | 3-5x/week | 3-4x/week |
| Content lifespan | 6-12 months (evergreen) | 24-72 hours (viral window) | 3-7 days |

### The Repurposing Pipeline (Extracted from Top Creators' Behavior)

Alex Hormozi's observed flow:
1. Record 1 long-form YouTube video (30-60 min)
2. Extract 5-8 short clips for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
3. Turn key frameworks into Instagram carousels
4. Pull quotes for X/Twitter threads
5. Every piece links back to Skool community

**Application for @big_quiv:** Record 1 deep-dive crypto strategy video per week. Extract 5 TikTok clips (each with a contrarian hook). Convert the framework into a carousel. Pull bold claims for X threads. Every piece drives to Telegram community or Krib membership.
