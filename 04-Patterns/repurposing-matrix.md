| Thread (X) | Read aloud as reel | Read aloud as short | Rewrite for LinkedIn tone | Original home | Adapt to carousel | Pin as list graphic | Rewrite as email | Expand to blog post |

## Adaptation Rules

- LinkedIn: add professional context, remove slang, longer intro
- TikTok: shorten to 30-45s, add trending sound, face-to-camera
- YouTube Shorts: same as TikTok but can be slightly longer (60s)
- Pinterest: static image with text overlay, link to full content
- Email: personal tone, add exclusive insight not in the public post
- Blog: expand with subheadings, examples, and internal links
