# Content Format Comparison

| Format | Effort | Reach | Save Rate | Authority | Completion Rate | Shelf Life | Repurpose Options |
|--------|--------|-------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|------------|-------------------|
| Studio Reel (60s) | High | Medium | High | Very High | Medium | Long | YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, blog embed |
| Cell Phone Reel (30-45s) | Low | Very High | Low | Low | High | Short | TikTok, Shorts, X video |
| Green Screen (45-60s) | Medium | Medium | Medium | High | Medium | Medium | TikTok, Shorts, LinkedIn |
| Carousel (5-10 slides) | Medium | Medium | Very High | High | High | Very Long | Pinterest, LinkedIn, blog, email |
| Thread (X) | Low | High | Medium | High | Medium | Medium | LinkedIn, blog, newsletter, carousel |

## Best Starter Combination

Cell Phone Reel + Carousel + Thread

These three cover high reach (cell phone), high saves (carousel), and high authority (thread) with minimum effort.
