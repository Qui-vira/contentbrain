## Format Patterns

| Format | Frequency | Avg Likes | Avg Views | Performance |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Single Tweet (standalone) | ~55% | 650 | 30K | High for motivational, medium for shills |
| Quote Tweet (own or others) | ~25% | 350 | 35K | Views high but engagement lower per like |
| RT of Own Content | ~10% | Redistributes original engagement | Extends reach | Used to resurface performing threads |
| Thread | ~5% | 1,352 (top thread) | 155K | Highest ceiling when it hits |
| RT of Others | ~5% | Low direct engagement | Low | Used for community building |

**Key finding:** Farmercist is overwhelmingly a single-tweet creator. He rarely writes threads. When he does (AI video thread), it massively outperforms everything else. His most common format is a short, punchy single tweet (1-3 sentences) that relies on personality and relatability rather than information density.

**Key insight:** Farmercist does NOT use "link in bio" CTAs. He embeds URLs directly in tweets with step-by-step instructions. His sponsored content follows a consistent formula: personal social proof opener -> project introduction -> step-by-step guide -> urgency close.

## What Gets 100K+ Views vs 20K Views

### 100K+ Views (3 posts)
1. **AI Video Thread** (155K views) - Thread format + money numbers in hook + step-by-step value + RT of own content
2. **$250 Airdrop Social Proof** (113K views) - "Free money" narrative + quote tweet of on-chain data + FOMO
3. **RealBet Shill** (46K views) - Personal social proof opener + detailed step-by-step + multiple value promises

**Pattern for viral:** Money numbers + proof screenshot + step-by-step guide = maximum views. The combination of social proof (I got paid) + actionable steps (here's how you can too) is his viral formula.

### 20K-50K Views (typical range)
- Trading profit flexes, airdrop breakdowns, sponsored shills with clear instructions
- These perform consistently but don't break out

### Under 20K Views
- Pure motivational content without crypto angle
- Personal life updates without money hook
- Short engagement questions (high replies but lower views)

**The gap:** His motivational content gets the highest engagement RATE (likes per view) but lower absolute views. His sponsored/shill content gets higher absolute views but lower engagement rate. His sweet spot is combining both: motivational opener + crypto opportunity.

## Posting Frequency

- Averages 3-5 original tweets per day
- Heavy RT of own content (self-amplification)
- Posts from iPhone (always mobile)
- Most active between 6am-12pm UTC (morning Lagos time)
- Weekend posting is slightly lower

## Engagement Triggers

1. **Money numbers** in hook (specific dollar amounts) -> drives views and bookmarks
2. **Short provocative statements** (under 15 words) -> drives replies
3. **Pidgin English** mixed with standard English -> drives relatability with Nigerian audience
4. **Health/fitness content** -> surprisingly high engagement, builds multidimensional persona
5. **"I got paid" social proof** -> drives FOMO and action on sponsored posts
