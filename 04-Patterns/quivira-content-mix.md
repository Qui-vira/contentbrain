## Rotation Rules

- Never post the same content type two days in a row
- At least 1 promo/CTA per week (minimum 3/month)
- At least 1 video per week
- Problem solver and tactical content should always include a CTA to a relevant product
- Authority content should cycle between: client proof, signals results, personal journey, market analysis
- Complete thought content should rotate narratives from 10-Niche-Knowledge/personal-brand/narratives.md
