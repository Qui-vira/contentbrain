# Content Goal Mapping

Quick reference table for matching content to business goals. The content-strategist and ghostwriter skills use this when planning and writing.

---

## Goal-to-Content Map

| Goal | Content Type | Format | Best Platform | Posting Frequency |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Sales | Problem-solution, client results, case studies, product breakdowns | Single tweet, LinkedIn post, thread | X, LinkedIn | 2x per week |
| Reach | Relatable stories, controversy, storytime, hot takes | Thread, TikTok script, Reel script | X, TikTok, Instagram | 2-3x per week |
| Leads | Value posts with CTA, lead magnets, free resources, Telegram funnel | Tweet with DM CTA, LinkedIn with link, carousel | X, LinkedIn, Instagram | 1-2x per week |
| Authority | Niche analysis, predictions, frameworks, data-backed takes | Thread, LinkedIn long-form, YouTube | X, LinkedIn, YouTube | 1-2x per week |
| Community | Audience Q&A, polls, shoutouts, niche news commentary | Single tweet, poll, quote tweet | X, Telegram | Daily |

## Weekly Cycle Template

| Day | Primary Goal | Secondary Goal | Platform Focus |
|-----|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| Monday | Authority | Sales | X thread + LinkedIn |
| Tuesday | Reach | Community | X storytime + TikTok |
| Wednesday | Sales | Leads | X promo + LinkedIn CTA |
| Thursday | Reach | Authority | X hot take + TikTok |
| Friday | Leads | Community | X lead magnet + poll |
| Saturday | Community | Reach | X engagement + memes |
| Sunday | Authority | Sales | X thread + LinkedIn reflection |

## Movement Multipliers (apply to any goal)

These compound everything. Layer them onto your regular content:

| Multiplier | How to Apply | Frequency |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Polarizing stand | State a strong position on a niche topic | 2x per week minimum |
| Shared mythology | Reference your origin story, turning points, brand language | 1x per week |
| Common enemy | Name what holds your audience back. Position your offer as the weapon. | 1x per week |
| Energetic delivery | Short sentences, punchy, human voice, street confidence | Every post |

## Content Mix Ratios

Weekly target:
- 30% Value/Education (authority + leads)
- 25% Personal Brand/Story (reach + mythology)
- 20% Engagement/Community (polls, replies, shoutouts)
- 15% Promotional (sales + CTAs)
- 10% Hot takes/Memes (reach + polarizing stands)

## The Growth Formula

Views create attention (reach content).
Sales create revenue (sales content).
Leads create options (lead gen content).
Expert positioning creates longevity (authority content).
Community creates loyalty (engagement content).

Repeat the cycle weekly. Growth becomes predictable.

Be the guide, not the salesperson.
