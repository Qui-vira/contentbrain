## 3. Hook Formula Patterns (from Transcript Analysis)

### Kallaway's 6 Hook Archetypes (282K views, from transcript)
1. **The Shock Hook** -- Open with something unexpected or counterintuitive
2. **The Story Hook** -- Start mid-action in a narrative
3. **The Curiosity Gap** -- Tease a reveal without giving it away
4. **The Challenge Hook** -- Dare the viewer or create stakes
5. **The Pattern Interrupt** -- Do something visually unexpected
6. **The Authority Hook** -- Lead with credentials or data

### Jenny Hoyos Method (from Jay Clouse interview, 4.6M views)
- Every second counts -- every single frame earns its place
- Test 3-5 hook variations per concept before publishing
- End the video right after delivering on the hook promise (no filler)
- "I don't ask if it'll go viral. I can figure out how to make it viral."

### Marcus Jones 2-Minute Hack (2M views, from transcript)
- Repost the same content with a new hook if it underperforms
- The video that got 4.5M views was dead at 11K views, then resurged after he optimized the hook
- The hook is the ONLY variable that matters for initial distribution

### Aprilynne Alter's 4-Step YouTube Intro (1.5M views)
1. Thumbnail alignment -- intro must match what the thumbnail promised
2. Credibility stamp -- fast proof you know what you're talking about
3. Value promise -- exactly what the viewer will learn
4. Stakes -- what happens if they don't watch

## 4. CTA Patterns

| CTA Type | Frequency | Platform | Performance |
|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| "Follow for more [topic] tips" | 45% of TikToks | TikTok | Moderate -- standard but low friction |
| "Save this for later" | 30% of TikToks | TikTok | High save rate, good for algorithm |
| Free resource link in description | 60% of YouTube | YouTube | High conversion -- PDF, template, tool |
| "Comment [keyword]" for DM funnel | 15% of TikToks | TikTok | Highest comment volume when used |
| Caption-only CTA (no spoken CTA) | 40% of TikToks | TikTok | Common for short (<15s) videos |
| "Subscribe" with bell animation | 25% of YouTube | YouTube | Standard, low differentiation |
| "Which one was your favorite?" | 20% across all | All | Best comment driver for listicle formats |

**Winning CTA pattern:** TikTok top performers use NO spoken CTA -- the content IS the pitch. YouTube top performers offer a free resource (PDF, template, hook database) as the CTA. The best-performing CTA across platforms is "Which one will you try?" in the caption.
