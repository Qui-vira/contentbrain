## VERSION 1 — Beginners Segment

> **Content Type:** COMMUNITY (question-led) | Goal: Leads | Structure: Open Loop | Slot: Afternoon or Evening
> **Audit note:** Correctly typed. Asks a question, sparks replies, does not teach and does not sell directly. The "Not selling yet" line is intentional — it disarms the audience. No mismatch.

**Platform:** X/Twitter + TikTok

>

## HOW TO USE THESE POSTS

1. **Publish all 4 versions on their respective platforms on April 17.**
2. **Read every reply within 2 hours** and respond to each one personally. This is market research, not broadcasting.
3. **Track what people say they want.** Note recurring words, objections, topics. Fr fr, these exact words become your launch copy.
4. **DM everyone who replies positively:** "Thanks for answering. We're opening early access in a few days. Want to be first?"
5. **Count the responses.** If you get fewer than 20 across all platforms, reconsider the launch timeline or push harder on the free content drops in Days 13–14.
6. **Green light signals:** Any combination of the following means proceed to full announcement (Day 17):
   - 20+ genuine replies across platforms
   - 5+ people asking "when does it start?"
   - 3+ people asking for the link before it exists
7. **Yellow light signals:** Fewer than 10 replies, mostly questions about price. Adjust messaging: emphasise value harder, drop a freebie first.
8. **Red light signal:** Fewer than 5 replies total. Delay announcement. Run another 3–5 days of pure value content before trying again.
