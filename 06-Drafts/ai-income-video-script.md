# AI Income — 60-Second TikTok/Reel Script

**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Format:** Talking head + text overlays + screen recordings
**Tone:** Calm, factual, no hype. Let the numbers do the talking.
**Music:** Low ambient beat, builds slightly at Tier 3-4 section

---

## SCENE 1: HOOK (0-3s)

**Visual:** Text slams onto screen with bass drop SFX: "47 AI PLATFORMS. ONLY 15 PAY."
**Camera:** Cut to close-up, direct eye contact.

**VO:**
"I researched forty-seven AI income platforms this week. Only fifteen actually pay from Nigeria."

---

## SCENE 2: RE-HOOK (3-7s)

**Visual:** Quick flash of spreadsheet/research notes on screen (screen record B-roll, 1 second). Back to talking head.

**VO:**
"Most AI money content is recycled garbage from people who never applied to a single one. Here's what actually works."

**Text overlay:** "VERIFIED APRIL 2026"

---

## SCENE 3: TIER 1 (7-15s)

**Visual:** Text overlay showing platform names + pay rates as speaker names them. Clean, dark background.

**VO:**
"Tier one — start today, zero experience. Mindrift pays five to twenty dollars an hour. Clickworker, five to twelve. TELUS Digital, ten to twenty. RemoteAfrica, twenty to twenty-five. All accept Nigerians. All pay via Payoneer."

**Text overlay:** Each platform name appears as mentioned.

---

## SCENE 4: TIER 2 — RE-HOOK (15-25s)

**Visual:** Slight camera angle change. Text overlay shifts color (red accent).

**VO:**
"But here's where it gets interesting. Tier two — learn prompt engineering in thirty days. Outlier AI pays fifteen to sixty per hour. xAI pays thirty-five to sixty-five. That's Elon Musk's company. No tech experience needed."

**Text overlay:** "$35-$65/hr — xAI" with red accent

**VO (pattern interrupt):**
"One warning though. Outlier has a non-payment history. Withdraw your money immediately. Never hold a balance."

---

## SCENE 5: TIER 3-4 (25-45s)

**Visual:** Energy shift. Slightly wider shot. Music builds.

**VO:**
"Now the real play. Tier three — sell AI services. Build chatbots: two to eight K a month. Prompt engineering consulting: fifty to two hundred an hour. AI video production: two to six K a month."

**Text overlay:** Dollar amounts appear as stated.

**VO:**
"Tier four — build a business. AI automation agency. Two retainer clients at three K each. That's six thousand a month recurring. This is the two thousand eighteen social media agency wave all over again. The window is open right now."

---

## SCENE 6: THE HARD TRUTH (45-52s)

**Visual:** Close-up. Serious tone. Music drops.

**VO:**
"Here's what nobody tells you. Same task, same quality — African workers get paid three to ten times less on these platforms. That's why tiers three and four matter. When you sell services, you set the rate. No algorithm decides you're worth less because of your location."

---

## SCENE 7: CTA (52-60s)

**Visual:** Text overlay: "COMMENT 'AI' FOR THE FULL LIST"

**VO:**
"Full research doc with fifty sources. Every rate verified. Every platform checked for Nigeria access. Comment 'AI' and I'll send it to you."

**Text overlay:** "47 platforms. 15 verified. 50+ sources."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **First 3 seconds are everything.** The text slam + bass drop must stop the scroll before the voice even starts.
- **No B-roll filler.** Every second is either data, a platform name, or a pay rate. Information density keeps retention.
- **The "hard truth" section at 45s is the re-hook** — it's the emotional peak that prevents drop-off before the CTA.
- **Caption should include:** "Full list of 15 verified AI platforms that pay from Nigeria. Every rate sourced. April 2026."
- **No hashtags** (per brand rules for X cross-post).
- **ManyChat keyword:** "AI" triggers DM with carousel + full doc link.
