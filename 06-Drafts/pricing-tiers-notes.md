## Pricing Rationale (for use in content)

Real talk: $15–$50 is below live cohort market rate ($97–$500 industry benchmark). This is intentional. Reasons to state publicly:
1. Maximise Cohort 1 enrolment → collect testimonials at scale
2. Build proof of community for Cohort 2 promotion
3. First cohort deserves founding prices — this closes May 2, prices go up after

> **Verification note:** "$97–$500 industry benchmark for live cohorts" — Source: Udemy Marketplace Report 2026, Skool community examples. Cite as industry range, not specific competitor.
