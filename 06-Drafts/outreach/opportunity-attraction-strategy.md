## PART 1: INBOUND ATTRACTION STRATEGY

### Personal Branding Angles

**Primary positioning:** "The AI Growth Builder" — the person who builds AI systems that replace entire marketing teams.

**Authority signals to establish:**
1. **Builder, not talker** — Show the actual systems you've built (ContentBrain, signal bot, video pipeline)
2. **Results-first content** — Every post leads with a measurable outcome ($X saved, Y% improvement, Z hours automated)
3. **Technical credibility** — Share architecture diagrams, code snippets, system dashboards
4. **Transparency** — Share real numbers (income reports, signal win rates, content performance)
5. **Social proof** — Document every win, client result, challenge entry, and bounty completion

**Content pillars for attraction:**
- "I built X with AI" (showcase posts) — 2x/week
- "How to automate Y" (education posts) — 2x/week
- "I just made $X from Z" (proof posts) — 1x/week
- "Your project is losing money because..." (pain point posts) — 1x/week
- "Behind the scenes of my AI engine" (transparency posts) — 1x/week

### Platforms to Focus On

| Platform | Purpose | Frequency | Content Type |
|----------|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **X/Twitter** | Primary reach + authority | Daily | Threads, single tweets, AI showcases |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B client acquisition | 3x/week | Long-form value posts, case studies |
| **TikTok** | Viral reach + younger audience | 3x/week | AI tool demos, before/after, tutorials |
| **Instagram** | Visual portfolio + community | 3x/week | Reels, carousels, stories |
| **Telegram** | Community + signal delivery | Daily | Signals, alpha, community engagement |
| **Binance Square** | Crypto-native audience | 3x/week | Trading content, signal showcases |

### Messaging That Converts Attention Into Leads

**Bio/headline formula:**
> "I build AI systems that replace marketing teams | ContentBrain creator | DM 'GROWTH' for a free AI audit"

**CTA rotation (use different CTAs across posts):**
1. "DM 'AUDIT' for a free AI growth audit of your project"
2. "Book a 15-min call — I'll show you what your AI engine could look like"
3. "Want me to build this for your project? Link in bio"
4. "Reply with your project name — I'll tell you what I'd automate first"
5. "Save this. You'll need it when you're ready to scale"

**Lead magnet ideas:**
- Free AI growth audit (15-min call, review their content/community, show quick wins)
- "AI Content Engine Blueprint" PDF (capture email/DM)
- Free ContentBrain demo (show the actual system running)
- "5 Things I'd Automate for Your Project" custom video (personalized outreach)

6. **Discord/Telegram** — Most Web3 projects have public communities where leaders are active
7. **Crunchbase** — Find recently funded companies + founder info
8. **AngelList/Wellfound** — Startup team pages with roles listed

## PART 3: DISCOVERY CALL STRUCTURE (15 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)
- "Thanks for jumping on. Quick context — I build AI automation systems for Web3 projects. Let me learn about [Project] first."

### Discovery (5 min)
Ask these questions:
1. "What's your biggest growth bottleneck right now?"
2. "How are you currently handling content creation?"
3. "How many people on your marketing/community team?"
4. "What platforms are you active on?"
5. "What's your monthly content budget currently?"

### Demo (5 min)
- Show ContentBrain running live (Telegram bot, signal output, content pipeline)
- Show a before/after comparison relevant to their answers
- "Here's what this would look like for [Project]..."

### Close (3 min)
- "Based on what you told me, I'd recommend [Package X]. It would [specific outcome]."
- "I have [X] spots open this month. Want me to send over a proposal?"
- "I can have a custom proposal in your inbox by tomorrow."

### Post-Call
- Send proposal within 24 hours
- Include: scope, deliverables, timeline, price, payment terms
- Follow up in 48 hours if no response

## PART 4: SERVICE DELIVERY FRAMEWORK

### Onboarding (Week 1)
1. **Brand capture session** (30 min) — Voice, tone, visual style, content guidelines
2. **Access setup** — Social accounts, Telegram/Discord, analytics, CMS
3. **System configuration** — Set up AI content engine with their brand voice, competitors, and keywords
4. **First batch** — Produce 10 posts for approval (establishes quality baseline)
5. **Approval workflow** — Agree on review process (async Slack/Telegram, or weekly call)

### Execution (Ongoing)
- **Monday:** Weekly content batch delivered (7-10 posts)
- **Wednesday:** Mid-week batch delivered (7-10 posts)
- **Friday:** Weekend content queued + weekly analytics summary
- **Bi-weekly:** 30-min strategy call (review performance, adjust strategy)
- **Monthly:** Full performance report + next month's strategy

### Retention & Upsell
- **Month 1:** Deliver consistent results, build trust
- **Month 2:** Propose adding a new channel or service (e.g., "Your TikTok is untapped — want me to add video production?")
- **Month 3:** Propose upsell to higher package or add-on services
- **Quarterly:** Full strategy review with ROI analysis

### Client Communication
- **Primary channel:** Telegram or Slack (async, fast)
- **Response time:** < 4 hours during business hours
- **Emergency:** Direct DM for urgent issues
- **Reporting:** Automated weekly reports via dashboard or PDF

## PART 5: PRICING & PAYMENT

### Payment Terms
- **Monthly retainers:** Paid upfront, first of each month
- **One-time builds:** 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- **Payment methods:** USDT, USDC, bank transfer, PayPal
- **Contract:** Simple 1-page agreement (scope, deliverables, timeline, price, termination clause)

### Pricing Anchors
When discussing price, anchor against alternatives:
- "A full-time content manager costs $4,000-$6,000/month. My AI engine does the same work for $2,000-$3,000."
- "A marketing agency charges $10,000-$20,000/month. I deliver more output at $3,000-$5,000."
- "This system replaces 3 tools ($500/month) + 2 people ($8,000/month) = $8,500. You're getting it for $3,000."
