A pattern I have noticed working with Web3 founders:

The ones who survive do not outwork everyone. They out-engineer everyone.

The grinders burn out in 6 months. The engineers build once and compound for years.

The difference is not talent. It is architecture.

If your revenue depends on you showing up every single day, you do not have a business. You have a performance.
