# Drone Pilot Outreach DMs

## Template A: Direct Opportunity

Hey! I saw your drone work and it's fire. We're building a drone photography marketplace app launching in Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, PH). Looking for 20 skilled pilots to join as founding members. Interested? I'll send details.

## Template B: Collaboration Angle

Your aerial shots are crazy good. Real talk, we're launching something big for drone pilots in Nigeria. A marketplace app connecting pilots with clients who need aerial content. Early pilots get priority bookings. Want in?

## Template C: Exclusive Access

No cap, your portfolio caught my eye. We're recruiting 20 drone camera pilots for a new marketplace app in Nigeria. Founding pilots get featured profiles and first access to paid gigs. Drop your email if you want the signup link.

---

## Usage Notes
- Send Template A to pilots with strong commercial portfolios
- Send Template B to pilots who post creative/artistic content
- Send Template C to pilots with large followings (1K+)
- Personalize the first line with a specific post or project reference
- All templates under 300 characters (core message)
- Follow up after 48 hours if no response
