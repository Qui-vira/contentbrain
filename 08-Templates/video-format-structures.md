# Video Format Structures

Three video format structures for short-form content production. Each has different specs, effort levels, and use cases.

---

## Format 1: Studio Reel (60 seconds)

Most polished format. Camera on tripod, screen recordings cut in, text overlays.

### Specs
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Duration: 60 seconds
- Tone: professional, authoritative
- Editing: Remotion or Premiere
- Word count: ~172 words at energetic pace

### Framework
Hook (0-3s) > Problem (3-10s) > Solution (10-40s) > Proof (40-50s) > CTA (50-60s)

### Production Table
| Time | Visual | Audio | On-Screen Text |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| 0-3s | Face close-up | Hook line | Hook text overlay |
| 3-10s | B-roll of problem | Describe problem | Key stat or pain point |
| 10-40s | Screen recording + face | Walk through solution | Step labels |
| 40-50s | Results screenshot | Show proof | Numbers/results |
| 50-60s | Face to camera | CTA | "Link in bio" / "Follow for more" |

### Required Shots
1. Face-to-camera hook delivery
2. Screen recording of process
3. B-roll of results/proof
4. CTA delivery with gesture

---

## Format 2: Cell Phone Reel (30-45 seconds)

Raw, one-take, shot on phone. No editing beyond captions. Gets the most reach because it feels native.

### Specs
- Shot on phone (front camera)
- No B-roll, no music, no cuts
- Duration: 30-45 seconds
- Word count: ~110 words
- Captions: auto-generated or 3 max overlays

### Shooting Instructions
| Setting | Instruction |
|---------|------------|
| Camera | Front-facing, phone propped or handheld |
| Framing | Head and shoulders, centered |
| Lighting | Natural light from window or ring light |
| Audio | Phone mic or clip-on lav mic |
| Takes | Record 2-3 takes, pick the most natural one |

### Script Format
One continuous spoken piece. Conversational. Like talking to a friend. No sections, no cuts, no transitions.

### On-Screen Text (max 3)
1. Opening: hook phrase (first 3 seconds)
2. Key moment: the one-liner that makes them save the video
3. CTA: what to do next

---

## Format 3: Green Screen Reel (45-60 seconds)

Stand in front of green screen with visual background (flowchart, diagram, tutorial). Higher authority than cell phone, faster to produce than studio.

### Specs
- Shot against green screen
- Edited in CapCut or Premiere
- Duration: 45-60 seconds
- Word count: ~130 words
- Background: diagram, chart, or step-by-step visual

### Shooting Instructions
| Setting | Instruction |
|---------|------------|
| Setup | Green screen behind, camera on tripod |
| Framing | Waist up, offset to one side (leave room for background visual) |
| Audio | External mic preferred |
| Gestures | Point to background elements as you reference them |

### Script Format
Script includes gesture cues: [point left], [gesture to chart], [tap on step 3]

### Editing Notes
- Chroma key: green screen removal
- Background: place diagram/chart behind speaker
- Text overlays: label each step as speaker mentions it
- Transitions: simple cuts between sections
