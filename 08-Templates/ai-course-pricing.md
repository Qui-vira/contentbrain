# Quivira AI Course — Pricing and Offer Structure

**Launch:** May 3, 2026 (Cohort 1)
**Enrolment window:** April 17–May 2, 2026
**Price range:** $15–$50 (below market rate for live cohort, deliberate founding price strategy)

---

## The Three Tiers

### TIER 1 — Starter ($15)
**Who it's for:** Self-paced learners, tight budgets, people testing quality before committing to live.
**What they get:**
- All pre-recorded modules for their selected topic + track
- PDF resource pack: prompt templates, workflow checklists, tool comparison guide
- Telegram community access for 30 days (read + post)
- Replay recordings after Demo Day
- NOT included: live classes, Q&A, certificate, multi-topic access

**Conversion hook:** "$15 and you get the full system: pre-recorded, at your own pace, with the templates we built it with."

### TIER 2 — Builder ($30)
**Who it's for:** Students who want accountability, live help, and classroom dynamic. The majority target.
**What they get:**
- Everything in Starter
- Live session attendance 2×/week (Tuesday + Thursday, 7–9PM WAT) via Google Meet
- Real-time Q&A with instructor
- Resource templates in all formats (Notion, PDF, Google Doc, raw prompts)
- Demo Day participation
- Completion certificate (branded Quivira PDF)
- Lifetime access to all recordings post-cohort
- NOT included: multi-topic access, direct DM to instructor, bonuses

**Conversion hook:** "$30 gets you live classes twice a week, Q&A, and a certificate. Not gonna lie, I've seen people pay $300 for less. First cohort price only."

### TIER 3 — Full Access ($50)
**Who it's for:** Serious builders wanting everything, direct access, and the bonuses. Business owners wanting max ROI.
**What they get:**
- Everything in Builder
- Access to ALL THREE course topics
- Direct DM to instructor: 1 question/week, 24hr response guarantee
- BONUS: Full ContentBrain System Walkthrough (never published elsewhere)
- BONUS: Trigon Labs Signal Setup Guide (how the AI trading signal system was built)
- Priority seating in all live sessions
- First access to Cohort 2 at founder rate (if they refer one student)

**Conversion hook:** "$50. Three courses. Direct access to me. Two bonuses I've never published. First cohort only. This will not be $50 again."

---

## Scarcity Mechanism
- Full Access capped at **20 students per cohort** (genuine. Direct DM access cannot scale beyond this)
- Communicate this clearly: "Not fake scarcity. I can only DM 20 people/week at quality."
- Track seats and post live count as it decreases

## Platform Stack
- **Gumroad:** International buyers (USD). Payment + download. Course content on linked Notion portal.
- **Selar.co:** Nigerian/African buyers (Naira). Same Notion portal link post-payment.
- **Notion portal:** Password-protected page with all pre-recorded module links.
- **Telegram:** Course community group (separate from signal group).
- **Google Meet:** All live sessions. Recurring links set up per track.

## Organic Promotion Per Tier

> **Content Type:** All promotion content below is PROMO. Content type audit: these posts name a product, state a price, and have a direct CTA. Correctly typed. No mismatch.
> **Exception flag:** LinkedIn case study ("I used to pay $2K/mo for a developer. Now I build it myself.") opens as PERSONALITY (Big Quiv's experience) to sell the Builder tier. If the body stays as a story with a soft CTA, this is PERSONALITY with PROMO tail. If it pivots to features/pricing, it is PROMO. Ghostwriter must classify at write time.

**Starter ($15):**
- X: "I'm charging $15 for this. I know what you're thinking." (thread, DM for access)
- Telegram: 30-second demo clip announcement
- Instagram: "I spent 2 years building this system. You're getting it for $15. Not a typo."
- Freebie: Give Module 1 free to prove quality before payment

**Builder ($30):**
- X: Walk through Day 1 content as a teaser thread → CTA: DM BUILDER
- Telegram: 60-second Kling video showing live session setup
- Instagram: Carousel — "What $30 gets you" — one slide per deliverable
- LinkedIn: Case study → "I used to pay $2K/mo for a developer. Now I build it myself."

**Full Access ($50):**
- X: "I'm giving away the ContentBrain system as a bonus. $50. Never been published."
- Telegram: "Full Access = direct DM access to me. This is the closest to 1-on-1 I've offered."

## Pricing Rationale
Real talk: $15–$50 is below live cohort market rate ($97–$500 industry benchmark). This is intentional:
- Maximise Cohort 1 enrolment for social proof and testimonials
- Collect testimonials at scale before raising prices
- Build proof of community for future cohort promotion
- Cohort 2 prices will be higher. Communicate this clearly in all promos.
