# Metrics Tracking with Benchmarks

| Metric | Where to Find | What It Tells You |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| Views | Platform analytics | How many people saw your content (reach) |
| Completion rate | TikTok/Reels analytics | How engaging the content is (retention) |
| Saves | Instagram/X analytics | How valuable the audience finds it (utility) |
| Shares/Reposts | All platform analytics | How shareable/relatable the content is (virality) |
| Comments | All platform analytics | How much conversation it sparks (engagement) |
| Keyword comments | Manual count or ManyChat | Lead magnet interest (conversion intent) |
| DMs sent | ManyChat analytics or manual | Lead capture success (funnel entry) |
| Link clicks | Bitly, UTM tracking, or ManyChat | Traffic driven to offer (conversion path) |
| New followers | Platform analytics | Growth from this specific content |
| Engagement rate | Calculate: (likes+comments+shares)/views*100 | Overall content quality signal |

## Success Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | Exceptional |
|--------|------|-------|-------------|
| Views (per post) | 500+ | 2,000+ | 10,000+ |
| Completion rate | 30%+ | 50%+ | 70%+ |
| Save rate | 2%+ | 5%+ | 10%+ |
| Share rate | 1%+ | 3%+ | 7%+ |
| Engagement rate | 3%+ | 5%+ | 10%+ |
| DMs from keyword | 5+ | 20+ | 50+ |
| Link click rate | 1%+ | 3%+ | 5%+ |
| Follower gain (per post) | 10+ | 50+ | 200+ |

## Tracking Instructions

Track weekly. Compare to previous week. Double down on what exceeds "Great" benchmarks. Stop what stays below "Good" for 2+ weeks.
