## How It Works

### SignalOS Flow
1. **SCAN** — Bot scans 20+ pairs every 4 hours (RSI, ATR, ICT, S/R, patterns)
2. **FILTER** — Only 3+ confluence signals pass. No noise.
3. **APPROVE** — Signals land in your private Telegram channel. Tap approve or reject.
4. **PUBLISH** — Auto-post to public Telegram + X/Twitter.

### ContentBrain Flow
1. **SCRAPE** — Pull posts from any competitor across 9 platforms
2. **EXTRACT** — Captions, metrics, hooks, transcripts, AI visual analysis
3. **ANALYZE** — Patterns, frameworks, CTAs, engagement correlations
4. **DISTRIBUTE** — Auto-save to organized vault folders
