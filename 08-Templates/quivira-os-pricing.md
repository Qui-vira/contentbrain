## Positioning Notes

- SignalOS is the entry point for traders — low friction, high value
- ContentBrain is the entry point for creators — solves content guesswork
- Quivira OS is the upsell for anyone doing both — bundled discount vs buying separately
- Agency tier exists for people who want to resell or manage multiple clients
