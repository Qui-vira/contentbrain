# Weekly Content Schedule Template

Use this template every week. The content-strategist skill references this when creating weekly content plans.

---

## MONDAY: Authority + Sales
Morning (X): Thread breaking down a niche topic with original analysis
Afternoon (LinkedIn): Long-form post with industry insight or prediction
Evening (Telegram): Share the thread link with commentary for Krib members

Goal split: 70% authority, 30% sales tie-in
Monetization: soft mention of relevant product at end of thread

---

## TUESDAY: Reach + Community
Morning (X): Storytime tweet or relatable moment
Midday (TikTok): Short video script (story format or hot take)
Evening (X): Reply to comments, engage with audience posts

Goal split: 60% reach, 40% community
Monetization: none. Pure awareness day.

---

## WEDNESDAY: Sales + Leads
Morning (X): Direct promo tweet for a product (Krib Signal, Ophir, consulting)
Afternoon (LinkedIn): Case study or client result post with CTA
Evening (Instagram): Carousel or Reel about the product with link in bio

Goal split: 50% sales, 50% leads
Monetization: direct CTA to product or lead magnet

---

## THURSDAY: Reach + Authority
Morning (X): Hot take or contrarian opinion about the niche
Midday (TikTok): Script reacting to a trending crypto/AI topic
Afternoon (X): Data-backed observation or framework share

Goal split: 50% reach, 50% authority
Monetization: none. Positioning day.

---

## FRIDAY: Leads + Community
Morning (X): Lead magnet tweet (free resource, DM keyword CTA)
Midday (X): Poll about audience struggles or interests
Evening (Telegram): Drop exclusive alpha or insight for Krib members

Goal split: 50% leads, 50% community
Monetization: lead capture through DM keyword or Telegram join

---

## SATURDAY: Community + Reach
Morning (X): Engagement bait, meme, or relatable quote
Afternoon (X): Respond to DMs and comments from the week
Evening: Rest or light engagement

Goal split: 60% community, 40% reach
Monetization: none. Relationship building day.

---

## SUNDAY: Authority + Sales
Morning (X): Reflective thread (weekly lesson, personal insight, or prediction)
Afternoon (LinkedIn): Professional reflection or industry observation
Evening: Plan next week's content using /content-strategist

Goal split: 60% authority, 40% sales tie-in
Monetization: thread ends with subtle CTA to product or community

---
