# 10 Hook Types by Format

| # | Hook Type | Description | Example |
|---|-----------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Trend Ride | Validate awareness of a current trend | "Everyone is talking about [trend]. Here is what they are missing." |
| 2 | Simplicity Promise | Remove the biggest barrier + time promise | "Set up [thing] in under 10 minutes. No experience needed." |
| 3 | Curiosity Gap | Make them feel like they are missing something | "There is one thing 99% of [audience] do not know about [topic]." |
| 4 | Bold Claim | Lead with the outcome, promise to show how | "I turned $500 into $5,000 using this exact system." |
| 5 | Direct Question | Call out the exact audience + empathize | "Struggling to grow on X with zero engagement? Read this." |
| 6 | Contrarian | Break the assumption holding the audience back | "Stop dollar-cost averaging. Here is what smart money does instead." |
| 7 | FOMO / Urgency | Social pressure + helpful rescue | "200 people already grabbed this guide. You are falling behind." |
| 8 | Before-After | Relatable pain + clear upgrade path | "6 months ago I had 200 followers. Today I have 15K. One thing changed." |
| 9 | Demo Tease | Promise a demo, create anticipation | "Watch me build a full trading dashboard in 3 minutes." |
| 10 | Authority | Credibility-first, establish trust | "After 3 years of building in Web3, here are the 5 things nobody tells you." |

## Format Matching Guide

For each hook, match to best format:
- Single tweet: Bold Claim, Direct Question, Contrarian, FOMO
- Thread: Authority, Before-After, Trend Ride
- LinkedIn: Authority, Simplicity Promise, Before-After
- TikTok/Reels: Demo Tease, Curiosity Gap, Bold Claim
- Carousel: Simplicity Promise, Before-After, Authority
