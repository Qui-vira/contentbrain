## Hook Formulas That Work for Design Content
| Formula | Example | Why It Works |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| "Things I wish I knew when I started [skill]" | @_alexpillow (376K plays) | Curiosity gap + relatability. Viewer thinks "what am I missing?" |
| "X tips I always keep in mind when [doing skill]" | @_bradmead (94K plays) | Authority framing. "Always" implies hard-won wisdom. |
| "Learn [complex skill] in X minutes" | @designvoodoo (3.7K plays) | Time-boxing reduces perceived effort. Makes complex seem accessible. |
| "[Tool] hack tutorial" | @worldofcanva (317K plays) | "Hack" implies shortcut/secret. Tool name attracts tool users. |
| Process reveal (show the making) | @carmellangdesigns (45K plays) | Behind-the-scenes is inherently engaging. People love watching creation. |

## Adaptation Notes for @big_quiv
