### Hook Formulas from Carousel Research (Cross-Niche)

These high-performing carousel hook formulas were identified from 2025-2026 Instagram research and are directly transferable:

1. **Myth-Buster:** Slide 1 states a common belief, Slide 2 says "MYTH!" then explains reality
   - Data niche: "You should learn every programming language" -> MYTH
   - Quivira: "You need $50k to start trading" -> MYTH

2. **Shock + Pain Point:** Lead with a surprising stat that highlights a problem
   - Data niche: "87% of data science projects never make it to production"
   - Quivira: "95% of retail traders lose money in their first year"

3. **Curiosity Gap:** Promise a reveal on the last slide
   - Data niche: "The one skill that separates $60k analysts from $120k analysts (last slide)"
   - Quivira: "The one indicator that separates profitable traders from gamblers (last slide)"

4. **Before/After Transformation:** Show the journey in slide 1
   - Data niche: "6 months ago I couldn't write a single line of SQL"
   - Quivira: "6 months ago I didn't know what a blockchain was"

5. **Contrarian + Proof:** Bold statement + evidence across slides
   - Data niche: "Excel is more powerful than Python for 80% of business analytics"
   - Quivira: "Spot trading beats perps for 90% of retail traders"

---

### Key Takeaway for @big_quiv

The data analysis niche is **massively under-optimized for hooks**. 80% of posts use generic descriptive hooks. The creators winning are those using bold claims, cheatsheet formats, and career transformation stories. This is identical to the opportunity in crypto/trading content -- most creators post generic chart screenshots. The playbook is clear: steal the cheatsheet carousel format, add bold/contrarian hooks, and apply it to trading, DeFi, and on-chain analysis content.
