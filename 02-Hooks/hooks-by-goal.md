# Hooks by Goal Type

Organized by business goal. The ghostwriter skill scans this file to match hooks to the content goal.

---

## SALES HOOKS
- "I made $[X] this month from [product]. Here is what I did differently."
- "3 people messaged me this week saying [result]. Here is what they all had in common."
- "[Number] students joined Ophir this month. Here is why they chose us over free tutorials."
- "Stop paying for signals that lose money. Here is what a real signal group looks like."
- "You do not need another course. You need [specific result]. Here is how I deliver it."
- "My clients went from [before state] to [after state] in [timeframe]. Here is the system."
- "This one change made my [product] 3x more effective. I am sharing it today."
- "I charged $[X] for this advice last week. Here it is for free. If it works, you know where to find more."

## REACH HOOKS
- "I almost quit crypto. Here is the thought that changed everything."
- "Nobody is talking about this, and it is going to be a problem."
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take about crypto/AI/trading]."
- "I lost [money/time/trust] because of [mistake]. Here is the lesson."
- "Everyone is doing [common thing]. Here is why it is wrong."
- "3 years ago I was [bad situation]. Today I [current result]. Here is what happened."
- "This is the most controversial thing I have ever said about [niche topic]."
- "I was wrong about [thing]. Here is what I know now."
- "The market humbled me. Here is why it was the best thing."
- "Delete this tweet if it offends you. But [bold statement]."

## LEAD HOOKS
- "I spent [time] building this [resource]. You can have it for free."
- "Drop '[keyword]' in my DMs and I will send you [lead magnet]."
- "This one resource changed how I [trade/build/create]. Here it is for free."
- "Join [number] people who already have this [guide/checklist/template]."
- "I am giving away [resource] to the first [number] people who [action]."
- "Free for the next 48 hours: [resource]. After that, it goes behind the paywall."
- "I made a checklist for [specific process]. 0 fluff. [number] steps. Comment '[keyword]' and I will DM it."
- "Everyone asks me how I [skill]. I wrote it down. Free. Link in bio."

## AUTHORITY HOOKS
- "Everyone is looking at this wrong. Here is what actually matters."
- "I analyzed [number] [things]. They all had [pattern] in common."
- "Here is my framework for [specific skill/analysis]. Use it."
- "I predicted [event]. Here is the signal nobody else was watching."
- "The data says something different from what CT is telling you. Let me show you."
- "[Number] years in [niche] taught me [insight]. Most people will not accept this."
- "I broke down [complex topic] into [number] simple steps. Thread."
- "The next big narrative is not [popular thing]. It is [your prediction]."
- "I spent [time] researching [topic]. Here are [number] things I found."

## COMMUNITY HOOKS
- "Someone asked me [question]. Here is my honest answer."
- "I keep getting DMs about [topic]. Let me address it publicly."
- "Poll: [question with 2-4 options]"
- "Shoutout to [member/student] who just [achievement]."
- "This news affects everyone in [niche]. Here is what you need to know."
- "What should I cover next? Your comments decide."
- "Quick question for my traders: [specific question about their experience]."
- "Raise your hand if you have ever [relatable struggle]. Let me help."

## MOVEMENT HOOKS (layer onto any goal)
- "If you believe [shared value], you belong here."
- "We do not [common bad practice] in this community. Here is what we do instead."
- "The [common enemy] wants you to stay confused. Here is how you fight back."
- "This is what separates [your community name] from everyone else."
- "I built [product/community] because I was tired of [enemy/problem]."
