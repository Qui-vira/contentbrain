## Tracking Notes

- Lead tracking: count DMs, email signups, and Telegram bot entries weekly
- Discovery calls: log in 00-Inbox/weekly reports or CRM
- Course sales: pull from payment platform dashboard
- Follower growth: pull from each platform's native analytics weekly
- Telegram: track via bot analytics or manual count
