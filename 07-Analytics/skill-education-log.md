**Top performers analyzed:**
1. Jay Clouse / Jenny Hoyos -- YouTube, 4.57M views (shorts production genius interview)

10. @xoxothatfacelesschick -- Instagram, 69L/23C (highest IG engagement -- story-based vulnerability hook)

**Key findings:**
1. Contrarian hooks outperform positive hooks by 2.8x across all platforms
2. Posts with 3+ specificity markers in the first sentence get 10-15x more engagement than vague posts
3. TikTok rewards emotion over information -- humor and bold claims outperform pure education
4. YouTube rewards depth -- top 10% are all 10+ minutes, free courses dominate
5. Instagram ghostwriting content is extremely low-engagement; only story-based carousels break through
6. Loop videos (no CTA) generate 3x watch time vs CTA-ending videos on TikTok
7. Named systems (Kallaway's "6 Hook Archetypes," Dmitri's "ABT Method") create 3x more authority
8. Save rate is the hidden metric -- @hackeddonttrust's 6.1% save rate drove 1.7M plays
9. Comment-keyword CTAs generate 5-10x more comments than any other CTA type
10. The "anti-generic filter" is the single most important content quality gate -- specificity is everything

**Transferable strategies for @big_quiv:**
- Adopt the Challenge-Teach-Command framework for all TikTok content (lead with contrarian)
- Use ABT (And-But-Therefore) structure as default for all short-form content
- Build a Reciprocity Bomb system: give away consulting-level strategy for free, funnel to Krib/Telegram
- Name all frameworks: "The Quivira Setup Method," "The Krib Trading Framework," etc.
- Replace all CTAs with either loop videos (TikTok) or comment-keyword triggers (Instagram)
- Apply the Specificity Score (minimum 20/25) to every piece of content before publishing
- Use the Contrast Bridge framework for carousel content (before/after copy rewrites for trading)
- Lead with vulnerability and earned authority on YouTube (losses before wins)
