# Signals Log

> Every signal generated by /technical-analyst is logged here. Used by /signal-tracker for performance calculations.

---

## Log Format

| # | Date | Pair | Direction | Timeframe | Entry | SL | TP1 | TP2 | TP3 | Confluence | Confidence | Status | Result | R Achieved |
|---|------|------|-----------|-----------|-------|----|-----|-----|-----|------------|------------|--------|--------|------------|

---

## Status Key

- **PENDING** — Signal generated, awaiting approval
- **ACTIVE** — Approved and live
- **TP1 HIT** — First take profit reached
- **TP2 HIT** — Second take profit reached
- **TP3 HIT** — Final take profit reached
- **STOPPED OUT** — Stop loss hit
- **CANCELLED** — Rejected or expired before entry
- **BREAKEVEN** — Closed at entry after TP1
