# Topic Research Hooks: Testimonial & Portfolio Page Design — 2026-03-30

## Result-First Hooks (Portfolio/Case Study Pages)
| Hook | Type | Engagement Pattern |
|------|------|-------------------|
| "How [client] increased revenue 340% in 90 days" | Result-first headline | Highest converting case study opener |
| "Results speak. Data, not claims." | Bold claim | Establishes authority, filters for serious buyers |
| "$2M+ revenue generated for 50+ clients" | Stats bar | Immediate credibility, CountUp animation |
| "The systems that produce real results" | Positioning | Authority + specificity |

## Social Proof Hooks (Testimonial Pages)
| Hook | Type | Engagement Pattern |
|------|------|-------------------|
| "Trusted by 500+ builders with a 4.9/5 average rating" | Aggregate stat | Wisdom of crowds — highest trust signal |
| "Don't take our word for it — see what clients say" | Trust redirect | Shifts authority from brand to customers |
| "Join 500+ builders who already..." | Bandwagon | FOMO + social proof combined |
| "See results like these? Let's talk." | CTA hook | Connects proof to action |

## Scarcity/Urgency Hooks (Real-time Social Proof)
| Hook | Type | Engagement Pattern |
|------|------|-------------------|
| "Sarah from London just booked a consultation" | Real-time notification | +98% conversion lift |
| "34 people viewed this in the last hour" | Activity counter | Creates urgency without being pushy |
| "Only 3 consultation slots left this month" | Scarcity | Loss aversion trigger |

## Before/After Hooks (Visual)
| Hook | Type | Engagement Pattern |
|------|------|-------------------|
| "Before: 200 followers. After: 12,000 in 3 months" | Transformation | Highest visual trust level |
| "Revenue: $0 → $10K/month in 60 days" | Metric transformation | Specific = credible |
