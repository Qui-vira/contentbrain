# Content Goal Framework

Every post serves one goal. Know the goal before you write. This framework maps content types to business outcomes for @big_quiv's niche (Web3, crypto, AI, trading).

---

## GOAL 1: SALES (Revenue)

What to talk about:
- The specific problems you solve
- Who you solve them for
- The results you have already produced

This builds trust and buying intent.

### Niche Examples
- "I helped 3 traders set up a risk management system that saved them from liquidation during the last BTC dump."
- "Ophir Institute students are now building full-stack dApps in 8 weeks. Here is exactly how the program works."
- "Hustler's Krib Signal members caught the $PEPE move 4 hours before CT. Here is the entry and exit."
- "My consulting clients go from zero presence to 10K followers in 90 days. Here is the system."

### Sales Hooks
- "I made $[X] this month from [product]. Here is what I did differently."
- "3 people messaged me this week saying [result]. Here is what they all had in common."
- "[Number] students joined Ophir this month. Here is why they chose us over free YouTube tutorials."
- "Stop paying for signals that lose money. Here is what a real signal group looks like."
- "You do not need another course. You need [specific result]. Here is how I deliver it."

### Ghostwriter Prompts
- "/ghostwriter write a sales tweet for Hustler's Krib Signal. Focus on a specific result members got this week."
- "/ghostwriter write a LinkedIn post selling Ophir Institute. Use a student success story."
- "/ghostwriter write a thread showing the exact process behind my consulting service. End with CTA."
- "/ghostwriter write a promo tweet for [product]. Use pain point > result > CTA structure."

### Metric to Track
DM inquiries, link clicks, signups, direct revenue

---

## GOAL 2: REACH (Attention)

What to lean into:
- Relatability
- Controversy
- Story times

These posts bring high views but low direct sales. Their job is awareness, not conversion.

### Niche Examples
- "I lost $2,000 on a shitcoin because I trusted a CT influencer. Here is what I learned."
- "Everyone is bullish on AI agents. Nobody is talking about the 90% that will fail. Here is why."
- "I was broke 3 years ago. Today I run a pharmacy, a blockchain institute, and a signal group. Here is the real story."
- "Unpopular opinion: 99% of Web3 courses are scams. Here is how to spot the 1% that are not."
- "The market humbled me last Tuesday. I sat in my car for 20 minutes. Here is the thought that changed everything."

### Reach Hooks
- "I almost quit crypto. Here is the thought that changed everything."
- "Nobody is talking about this, and it is going to be a problem."
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take about crypto/AI/trading]."
- "I lost [money/time/trust] because of [mistake]. Here is the lesson."
- "Everyone is doing [common thing]. Here is why it is wrong."
- "3 years ago I was [bad situation]. Today I [current result]. Here is what happened."
- "This is the most controversial thing I have ever said about [niche topic]."

### Ghostwriter Prompts
- "/ghostwriter write a storytime tweet about a trading loss and what I learned."
- "/ghostwriter write a controversial take about AI agents in crypto. Make it polarizing."
- "/ghostwriter write a relatable thread about the struggles of building in Web3."
- "/ghostwriter write a story post about my journey from zero to running multiple businesses."

### Metric to Track
Views, shares, reposts, new followers, impressions

---

## GOAL 3: LEADS (Pipeline)

What to create:
- Value posts with a clear CTA
- Lead magnets
- Free resources that require opt-in
- Telegram funnel entry points

### Niche Examples
- "I wrote a free guide: 5 trading mistakes that cost beginners $10K+. Drop 'GUIDE' in my DMs and I will send it."
- "Join my free Telegram group. Every Monday I drop one alpha trade setup with full analysis."
- "I created a checklist for airdrop farming. 0 fluff. 12 steps. Comment 'AIRDROP' and I will DM it."
- "Free workshop this Friday: How to build your first smart contract in 2 hours. Link in bio to register."

### Lead Hooks
- "I spent [time] building this [resource]. You can have it for free."
- "Drop '[keyword]' in my DMs and I will send you [lead magnet]."
- "This one resource changed how I [trade/build/create]. Here it is for free."
- "Join [number] people who already have this [guide/checklist/template]."
- "I am giving away [resource] to the first [number] people who [action]."

### Ghostwriter Prompts
- "/ghostwriter write a lead gen tweet offering a free trading checklist. CTA is DM with keyword."
- "/ghostwriter write a LinkedIn post promoting a free Telegram group. Focus on what they get every week."
- "/ghostwriter write a thread giving 80% value on a topic, then CTA to get the full guide."
- "/ghostwriter write a tweet promoting a free workshop. Include date, topic, and registration link."

### Metric to Track
Opt-ins, DM count, Telegram joins, email signups

## GOAL 4: AUTHORITY (Expert Positioning)

What to create:
- Niche commentary on current events
- Predictions backed by data or logic
- Deep analysis that shows expertise
- Original frameworks and mental models

### Niche Examples
- "Everyone is comparing L2s by TPS. That is the wrong metric. Here is what actually matters for adoption."
- "I analyzed 50 failed crypto projects. They all made the same 3 mistakes."
- "Here is my framework for evaluating any DeFi protocol in under 5 minutes."
- "The next cycle will not be driven by retail. Here is what the on-chain data says about institutional positioning."
- "I predicted the $SOL run 6 weeks before it happened. Here is the exact signal I used."

### Authority Hooks
- "Everyone is looking at this wrong. Here is what actually matters."
- "I analyzed [number] [things]. They all had [pattern] in common."
- "Here is my framework for [specific skill/analysis]. Use it."
- "I predicted [event]. Here is the signal nobody else was watching."
- "The data says something different from what CT is telling you. Let me show you."
- "[Number] years in [niche] taught me [insight]. Most people will not accept this."

### Ghostwriter Prompts
- "/ghostwriter write an authority thread breaking down how to evaluate DeFi protocols."
- "/ghostwriter write a LinkedIn post with a prediction about the next crypto cycle. Back it with logic."
- "/ghostwriter write a tweet sharing an original framework for [topic]. Make it saveable."
- "/ghostwriter write a data-backed analysis of [trend] using on-chain metrics."

### Metric to Track
Saves, bookmarks, quote tweets, follower quality, brand deal inquiries

---

## GOAL 5: COMMUNITY (Loyalty)

What to create:
- Content from questions people keep asking you
- Polls and discussion starters
- Niche issue commentary
- Audience shoutouts and win celebrations

### Niche Examples
- "Someone asked me: 'Is it too late to learn Solidity?' Here is my honest answer."
- "Poll: What is your biggest struggle in crypto right now? A) Finding good projects B) Managing risk C) Building consistency D) Understanding the tech"
- "Shoutout to @[member] who just deployed their first smart contract through Ophir. This is what we do."
- "The SEC ruling this week affects every builder in Web3. Here is what it means for you."
- "What do you want me to break down next? Drop your topic below."

### Community Hooks
- "Someone asked me [question]. Here is my honest answer."
- "I keep getting DMs about [topic]. Let me address it publicly."
- "Poll: [question with 2-4 options]"
- "Shoutout to [member/student] who just [achievement]."
- "This news affects everyone in [niche]. Here is what you need to know."
- "What should I cover next? Your comments decide."

### Ghostwriter Prompts
- "/ghostwriter write a community tweet answering a common question about trading."
- "/ghostwriter write a poll tweet about struggles in Web3. 4 options."
- "/ghostwriter write a shoutout tweet celebrating a student win from Ophir."
- "/ghostwriter write a LinkedIn post commenting on a recent crypto news event."

### Metric to Track
Comments, replies, poll votes, DMs, community retention rate
