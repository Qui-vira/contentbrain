## POSTING FORMULA

### The Farmercist Content Loop (Daily)

1. **Morning motivational** (6-8am UTC) -- Short wisdom drop, 1-3 sentences, no links
2. **Mid-morning alpha** (9-11am UTC) -- Airdrop breakdown, opportunity post, or project shill
3. **Afternoon engagement** (12-3pm UTC) -- Question, callout, or banter post
4. **Evening RT** -- Retweet own best-performing content from earlier or previous days

### Tweet Structure Formula

**For Motivational:**
- Line 1: Bold statement (under 15 words)
- Line 2-3: Expansion with context
- Line 4: Close with "Lock in" / emoji

**For Sponsored Shills:**
- Line 1: Personal social proof ("I just got...", "I got $X from...")
- Lines 2-5: Project introduction with stats (live since X, Y users, Z paid out)
- Lines 6-10: Step-by-step guide (visit, connect, complete, earn)
- Close: Urgency + tag project

**For Engagement:**
- Single line question OR bold callout
- No links, no images
- Let the replies do the work
