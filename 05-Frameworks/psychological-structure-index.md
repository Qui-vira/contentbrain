# Psychological Structure Index — Retention Patterns That Keep Watch Time High

> **Source of truth for script structure.** Ghostwriter and concept skills read this when building video scripts.
> Last rebuilt: 2026-04-01 | Total structures: 44 | Scoring: 4-criteria rubric

---

## Selection Algorithm

When a skill builds a video script or content piece:

1. **IDENTIFY** the content goal (Sales/Reach/Authority/Community/Leads)
2. **FILTER** structures by Goal match
3. **PICK** the structure that matches the emotional arc needed
4. **LAYER** it into the script outline BEFORE writing dialogue
5. The structure defines WHEN tension rises, WHEN payoff hits, WHERE open loops go
6. Do NOT pick the same structure for consecutive posts on the same platform

---

## Scoring Rubric (4 Criteria x 5 Points = /20, normalized to 0-100)

| Criterion | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Average) | 5 (Strong) |
|-----------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **RETENTION PULL** | No specific retention mechanism | Keeps some viewers past 3s | Keeps viewers watching past 3s, 7s, and 15s marks |
| **EMOTIONAL ARC** | Flat energy throughout | Mild rise and fall | Rising tension that resolves satisfyingly |
| **ADAPTABILITY** | Only works for one topic type | Works across 2-3 content types | Works across all topics and formats |
| **PROVEN BASIS** | Gut-feel invention | Based on observed pattern | Documented psychological principle with engagement data |

## COMPETITOR-PROVEN TIER (80-100)

**PS-001** | Score: 95/100 | Tier: COMPETITOR-PROVEN
Pattern: Outcome First, Then Rewind
How It Works: First 3 seconds show the finished result (the content calendar, the trade P&L, the published post). THEN rewind and show how you got there. The outcome grabs attention; the process builds desire.
Script Structure:
  - 0-3s: Flash the impressive end result (screenshot, number, visual proof)
  - 3-7s: "Here's how I did it" or "Let me show you the process"
  - 7-15s: Step 1 of the process, quick and visual
  - 15-30s: Steps 2-3, building momentum
  - 30s+: Final step + CTA ("Want the same result? Comment [KEYWORD]")
Goal Match: Sales/Authority
Best For: short-form
Source: ai-product-selling-framework.md

**PS-002** | Score: 95/100 | Tier: COMPETITOR-PROVEN
Pattern: Pain-Bridge-Solution
How It Works: Open by describing the prospect's specific pain and its consequences. Only after they feel the pain, introduce the solution as relief. The solution arrives as emotional release to tension you built.
Script Structure:
  - 0-3s: Name the pain directly ("You're losing money because...")
  - 3-7s: Agitate with specifics and consequences
  - 7-15s: Bridge: "So what we do is..."
  - 15-30s: Show the solution in action with proof
  - 30s+: Quantify the win + CTA
Goal Match: Sales/Leads
Best For: both
Source: pain-bridge-solution-dm-framework.md

**PS-003** | Score: 90/100 | Tier: COMPETITOR-PROVEN
Pattern: Zeigarnik Effect (Open Loop Stack)
How It Works: Unfinished tasks occupy the mind more than completed ones. Open 2-3 loops in the first 7 seconds that only close at the end. "You're 80% done" creates irresistible pull to finish watching.
Script Structure:
  - 0-3s: Open loop 1 (bold claim without proof)
  - 3-7s: Open loop 2 (tease "but first, you need to know...")
  - 7-15s: Deliver value that partially closes loop 2
  - 15-30s: Build toward the payoff, partially close loop 1
  - 30s+: Close all loops with the reveal + CTA
Goal Match: Reach/Authority
Best For: both
Source: marketing-psychology.md
