# Carousel Framework

## Specs
- Size: 1080x1350 pixels (4:5 ratio)
- Slide count: 5-10 slides
- Style: dark background, clean typography, red accents (Quivira brand)

## Slide Structure

| Slide | Purpose | Content |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Cover | Hook headline (adapted from hook types). Must stop the scroll. |
| 2 | Context | The "what" and "why" in 2-3 sentences. Why this matters to the reader. |
| 3-7 | Steps | Core tutorial in checklist format. 3-5 items per slide. Each item is a direct instruction. |
| 8 | Proof | Outcome stat, result, or testimonial. |
| 9 | CTA | "Comment [KEYWORD] and I will DM you the full guide" or "Follow for more" |

## Checklist Slide Rules
- Each item must be a direct instruction the viewer can follow
- Good: "Go to code.visualstudio.com" "Click the Extensions icon" "Search for Claude Code"
- Bad: "Install the tool" "Set things up" "Get started"
- Use checkboxes or numbered items
- One action per line
- No paragraphs, no long explanations
