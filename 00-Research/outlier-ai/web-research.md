## Source 1: RemoWork Blog — "Outlier AI Review: Getting Started, Tasks & Pay"
**URL:** https://remowork.life/blog/outlier-ai-review-a-complete-guide-to-getting-started-tasks-and-pay
**Date:** 2026

### What is Outlier AI
- Platform operated by **Scale AI** (founded 2016 by Alexandr Wang and Lucy Guo, HQ San Francisco)
- Connects skilled professionals with AI training tasks
- Focus: RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) for LLMs
- Scale AI valued at **$14 billion**
- Distributed **$100M+** to ~40,000 contributors globally

### Registration (7 Steps)
1. Visit outlier.ai — create account via email or Google; provide name, phone, DOB, address
2. Upload resume + connect LinkedIn
3. Identity verification — government ID, 1-2 business days
4. Select up to 10 expertise areas
5. General Reasoning Screening — must score **80%+**
6. Project-specific training
7. Start working — no minimum hours

### Pay Rates

|---|---|
| AI Content Editor/Writing Evaluator | $15–$35/hr |
| Freelance Writer | ~$25/hr |

| Coding Expert | $25–$50/hr |
| Software Engineer | ~$43/hr |
| Voice Acting for AI | Up to $40/hr |
| Legal Professionals (US) | $50–$100/hr |

### Payment
- Weekly on Tuesdays
- Work period: previous Tuesday–Monday (midnight UTC)
- Methods: PayPal, ACH bank transfer, Airtm

### Task Types
1. Response Evaluation & Ranking
2. Content Rewriting & Improvement

## Source 4: Outlier Official Blog — Onboarding Process
**URL:** https://outlier.ai/blog/your-first-steps-on-outlier-what-to-expect-from-onboarding
**Date:** 2026

### Timeline
- Resume & Profile Setup: 10-15 minutes
- Skill Selection & Screening: 15-30 minutes
- ID Verification: 5-10 minutes (processing: 1-2 days)
- Full project access: within 1-2 days max

### ID Verification
- Uses **Persona** (trusted by major financial institutions)
- Must be 18+
- No existing Outlier account
- Non-expired ID from working country
- Well-lit, clearly legible photos
- Accepted: driver's licenses, state IDs, passports, permanent resident cards, employment authorization cards, Sharecode (UK)

### Skill Verification
- Two-step: Selection Phase → Verification Phase (brief video assessments)
- "Authentic expertise always performs better than inflated claims"
- Max 10 skills — only pick ones you're truly comfortable with
- Technical skills validated via GitHub, Google Scholar, academic email

### Performance
- "Performance snowball effect" — early success leads to better opportunities
- Passed screenings = more consistent project flow

## Source 8: Common Mistakes / Account Risks
**Multiple Sources:**

### Reasons for Account Bans
- Quality score drops below 90-95% threshold
- Copying/pasting or using third-party automation tools
- Even careful correction flagged as "cheating" by automated review
- Speed vs quality catch-22

### Platform Risks
- No notification when removed from project — "door quietly shut"
- No appeals process for quality-related bans
- Algorithmic management with minimal human oversight
- Unpaid training can be lengthy
- Some report payment discrepancies
