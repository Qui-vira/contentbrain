# Research Synthesis: Outlier AI

## Key Findings Summary
1. Outlier AI is a legitimate Scale AI subsidiary paying $15-$100/hr for AI training tasks, with $100M+ distributed to 40,000+ contributors
2. Registration requires degree, government ID, and 80%+ on reasoning screening — onboarding takes 30-90 minutes but project assignment can take weeks
3. Payment is weekly on Tuesdays via PayPal or Airtm — no Payoneer or direct bank for most regions
4. Nigeria/Africa users face geo-restrictions: Nigerian phone numbers rejected, proxy/VPN/RDP needed, but Yoruba/Hausa language projects exist
5. As of April 2026, the Aether assessment exam is temporarily suspended for new registrations — timing matters

## Contradictions Resolved

### "Pays daily" vs "Pays weekly on Tuesdays"
- @silverpenydr lists Outlier as "pays daily" but every other source (including Outlier official, Glassdoor, multiple Twitter users) confirms **weekly on Tuesdays**
- **Resolution:** Weekly on Tuesdays is correct. "Daily" likely refers to daily task availability.

### "Not available in Nigeria" vs "Yoruba/Hausa projects available"
- @sleektru and @remotemondays say not available in Nigeria
- But Built In job listing shows "Remote AI Training – Yoruba Writers" by Outlier
- **Resolution:** General registration blocked for Nigerian IPs/numbers, but specific language projects (Yoruba, Hausa) ARE available. General access requires proxy/VPN/RDP workaround.

### RDP vs VPN
- @ObaDeleke recommends RDP over VPN
- Other tweets mention VPN (NSocks, CleanLTE)
- **Resolution:** RDP provides real machine fingerprint + US IP (better), VPN only changes IP (can be detected). RDP is preferred but more expensive.

### Pay range: $7/hr vs $100/hr
- @silverpenydr mentions OneForma at $7/hr (NOT Outlier)
- @sleektru says Outlier starts at $12/hr
- Review sites say $15-$100/hr
- **Resolution:** Outlier itself pays $12-$100/hr. The $7/hr is OneForma (a competitor). Entry-level Outlier tasks start ~$12-15/hr, specialists can earn $50-100/hr.
