## Extracted Techniques

### Visual Hooks (for visual-hook-index.md)
- V-NEW-014: Action-While-Teaching — Open with a casual physical action (pouring coffee, cooking, walking) while delivering content. The motion creates visual interest that static talking-head lacks. The mundane action makes the educational content feel organic, not scripted.
- V-NEW-015: Room-Hop Scene Changes — Cut between 3-4 different rooms/locations within a single short video. Each room has different colors, lighting, and energy. The visual variety IS the retention strategy.
- V-NEW-016: Prop Swap Re-Hook — Introduce a new physical prop at each scene change (coffee > laptop > pointing finger > close-up). Each prop gives the viewer something new to look at.

### Camera/Delivery Styles (for talking-head-style-index.md)

- TH-NEW-009: Multi-Room Progressive Close-Up — Start with medium shot in room 1, then cut to tighter medium in room 2, then close-up in room 3. Each room gets closer to the face, building intimacy.
